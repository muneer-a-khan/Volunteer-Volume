# Volunteer-Volume
ENGR 1020 - Volunteer Volume Group 10

## Problem Definition
We have a wonderful roster of volunteers at the museum. All sign up through our
Marketing & Outreach Manager and must communicate with her regarding shifts and duties. All
volunteers much also log their hours via paper timesheets. It would be great to come up with a
way to maintain the volunteer database and hours online that is user friendly for volunteers of all
ages.
